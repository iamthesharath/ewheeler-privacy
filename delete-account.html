EWHEELER – Delete Account Request

If you wish to delete your EWHEELER account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to ewheelerapp4u@gmail.com with the subject line:

Account Deletion Request – EWHEELER

Include the following in your email:

  • The Google account email address used to sign in to EWHEELER
  • A brief reason for deletion (optional)

We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Data Is Deleted

  • Your user profile (name, email, profile photo)
  • Your saved EV charging station preferences
  • Your app activity and usage data stored in Firebase

What Data May Be Retained

  • Anonymous usage statistics that cannot be linked back to you
  • Data required by law to be retained (if applicable)

For questions, contact us at ewheelerapp4u@gmail.com.
